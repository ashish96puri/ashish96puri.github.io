Ops Co‑pilot

Triage, route, and resolve faster. The co‑pilot summarizes context, proposes safe actions, and keeps an audit trail of every step.

Problem

  • Ops dashboards are noisy. Important work hides in queues.
  • Manual checks waste time and introduce inconsistency.
  • Compliance requires auditable decisions.

Solution approach

  1. Ingest cases across tools. Normalize events and entities.
  2. LLM suggests next actions with confidence and rationale.
  3. Guardrails enforce policy, SLAs, and role permissions.
  4. One‑click bulk actions with preview and rollbacks.
  5. Dashboards: throughput, stuck cases, exception reasons.

Reference architecture

Ingress, policy guard, action executor, and audit log.

Outcomes

  • −25–40% handle time on routine cases
  • +15–25% SLA adherence due to early triage
  • 100% action history with reason codes

Stack

Event bus, policy engine, safe tool layer, evaluator, and audit store. EU deployment by default.
