FAQ

Which models do you use?

We choose the model based on your needs: speed, privacy, cost, and accuracy. This can be a commercial API, an open-source model such as Llama or Mistral, or a model we run on your own servers.

Do you keep our data?

No. The work happens inside your cloud accounts and code repositories. Any sample data on my side is anonymized or synthetic and deleted after delivery.

What does success look like?

Before building, we agree on a few measurable targets and safety rules. The pilot rolls out to a small group first and must pass tests before a wider launch.

How do we start?

Book a 20‑minute intro. If there’s a fit, we run the one‑week diagnostic to produce a concrete plan and quote for the 30‑day pilot.
